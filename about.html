About Our Clinic

Excellence in Dental & Aesthetic Care Since 2010

Our Story

Dubai's Premier Dental & Skin Polyclinic

Invisible Braces Dental and Skin Polyclinic LLC was established with a vision to provide world-class dental and aesthetic treatments in the heart of Dubai. Located in the prestigious World Trade Center area, we have been serving the community with dedication and excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge technology with the expertise of internationally trained specialists. We believe in a patient-centered approach, where every treatment plan is customized to meet individual needs and expectations.

From invisible braces to advanced skin treatments, we offer comprehensive solutions under one roof, making us your one-stop destination for dental and aesthetic care in Dubai.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dental and aesthetic care using the latest technology and techniques, while maintaining the highest standards of patient comfort and satisfaction. We strive to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading dental and aesthetic polyclinic in the UAE, known for our innovative treatments, compassionate care, and commitment to excellence. We aim to transform lives through beautiful smiles and healthy skin.
