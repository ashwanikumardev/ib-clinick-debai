Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us with your insurance details for verification.

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to minimize wait times and ensure availability.

Is parking available?

Yes, ample parking is available at the World Trade Center complex. Parking validation is provided for patients.

What languages do you speak?

Our multilingual staff speaks English, Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, and other languages to serve our diverse clientele.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most treatments. Discuss options with our team during your consultation.

How do I prepare for my first visit?

Bring your ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records. Arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork.
